Number Traversal

Major Operators used

Modulus (%)

  • Peeche ki digit k liye
  • Gives remainder
  • Example
    let num = 786
    console.log(num%10)
    Output 6 (remainder or last digit)

Divide (/)

  • Aage ki digit k liye
  • number todna or number chhota karna
  • Gives quotient
  • Example
    let num = 786
    console.log(num/10)
    Output 78.6
    floor value = 78
    ceil value = 79
